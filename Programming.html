Food Alphabets

(Final Design)

(Mood board)

Title


The inspiration behind of the food alphabets is due to my
love for food. When starting on the first few letters of
the alphabet, I thought about how I could make the fonts
look eye-catching and unique, so I decided to use food to
represent letters, since they have different colors, and
there’s a large variety of food I can pick out to represent
the letters in the alphabet. Most of the food I used in
the design are my favourite snacks or food, such as the
chips, sushi and ice cream. During the design process, I
realized that some letters are difficult to represent, such
as the letter “Y”, so I thought about using an ice cream
that somebody took a large bite out of to create the
space between the 2 lines. Likewise, for stacking up sushi
rolls to form the letter “L”.
